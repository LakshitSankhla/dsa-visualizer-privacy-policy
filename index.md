# Privacy Policy
**DSA Visualizer**

**Effective Date:** February 26, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026  

## 1. Overview

DSA Visualizer is an educational app for learning data structures and algorithms through interactive visualizations.

We do not require account creation. Most app data is stored locally on your device.

## 2. Data We Process

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device
- Learning progress and completion status
- App settings and preferences
- Bookmarks and related in-app state
- Personal notes you write against individual algorithms (free-text, stored locally only)

This local data is used only to provide app features and personalization inside the app.

### 2.2 Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads in the free version of the app.  
When ads are enabled, Google may process data such as:
- Advertising ID (or similar device identifiers)
- Device/app information
- Approximate location inferred from IP address
- Ad interaction and performance events

AdMob data processing is governed by Google policies:
- https://policies.google.com/privacy
- https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

## 3. Consent and Ad Controls

Depending on region and applicable law, the app may request consent for ad personalization and ad data processing before requesting ads.

You can control ads by:
- Using **Settings > Privacy & Ads > Ad privacy options** (where available)
- Managing ad preferences at the device/account level

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We share limited data only as needed to operate the app, primarily with:
- Google AdMob (advertising)

## 5. Data Retention

- Local app data remains on your device until you reset app data or uninstall.
- Ad-related data is retained by Google based on Google's own retention policies.

## 6. Security

We use platform-provided protections and standard app security practices. No method of storage/transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 7. Age Requirements and Children's Privacy

**DSA Visualizer is intended for users aged 13 and older.**

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app or provide any information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us immediately at sankhla.lakshit27@gmail.com and we will delete such information promptly.

**COPPA Compliance:**
- We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- We do not knowingly serve personalised ads to users under 13
- Consent mechanisms are provided where required by law

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding access, deletion, objection, or restriction of data processing.

Because most app data is local, you can remove it directly by resetting in-app data or uninstalling the app.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when app features, legal requirements, or third-party integrations change. Updates will be reflected by the **Last Updated** date.
